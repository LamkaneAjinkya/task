Who We Are And What We Do

History

In 2018, AL Constructions Materials will be celebrating a huge milestone – a 10th anniversary. This flourishing business has been family-owned and family-operated from its earliest days, a point of great pride for the Lamkane family.

In the 2008’s, Mashesh lived in Solapur, Maharashtra; he was working for a local construction supply house. After a year and a half of working the counter, he was promoted to management in the construction department - complete with a new car, expense account and sales commissions. The General Contractors who were their customers were regularly approached with low prices but little customer service. Keeping that commission in mind, Mashesh made note of the deficit in service and tried a better approach.

Service became the name of his game. Picking up coolers, coffee cups or supplies for job locations, Mashesh made a point of trying to think of ways to meet service needs before even asked; he made himself invaluable. Within 2-3 years, his sales rapidly increased, and Mashesh cast his eyes toward his own entrepreneurship.
